Back to 1995 the founder of port museum start collecting rarest tables from all around the world, his passion and love to this art never stop here until he opens a museum for public to share with them his passion and show the world this rare art that start disappearing. Port museum want to take those people who lost their sols inside painting and art to educate them the importance of these tables and teach them about the ancient history.Inside port museum you only will feel like a lost scene in history documentary .
